How to play Hexicography

First click the letter in your rack that you want to play...

...then click the blank spot on the board where you want to play it. (You can drag it there too.)

Now spell out a word by dragging from beginning to end. The word needs to use your new letter. (If you can’t find a word, just click End turn.)

Playing a word captures its tiles and changes them to your color. If a tile is already yours, you’ll reinforce it — your opponent will have to change it twice to steal it back.

Once the board is full, whoever controls more tiles wins!
